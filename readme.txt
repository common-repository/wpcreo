== Description ==

Transform your WordPress into an iOS, Android and Windows apps without writing a single line of code. 
By installing WPCREO wordpress plugin you will be able to convert your WordPress content automatically into a customize app. 
The plugin comes with stylish themes. 
You can change all aspects of the theme including theme colours, your logo, app icons, fonts, and social media sharing. 
Through the settings area you can change the number of posts that are displayed and exclude specific categories and tags. 
The home page of the app design features a beautiful tile system. It can display all posts, posts from a specific tag, or posts from a specific category. 
You can also specify the exact posts and pages that are displayed on the app home page.

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the WPCREO plugin and get your app built and submitted.

1.	Start by installing WPCREO WordPress plugin 
2.	Sign up for one of our pricing plans or get a FREE Sandbox-account http://www.wpcreo.com/#plan-price
3.	login to your WPCREO account > Click on your “Publisher Information” and Copy your WPCREO “WordPress plugin URL”
4.	Go back to your WordPress “Settings” menu and click on “WPCREO”, Paste the URL and click on “save settings”
5.	Download the WPCREO Sandbox app for your desired platform from iTunes, Android or Windows 8.1 
6.	That's it! All your post and pages will be automatically transfer to a specific category on your app home page.
